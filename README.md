# Track-Hub

Track-Hub is an AI-powered development assistant that helps you understand codebases, analyze repositories, and transcribe meetings.



## How it works

- **Connect Repository**
    - Enter the URL of your GitHub repository to connect it to Track-Hub. Our AI will summarize recent commits and analyze the codebase for insights.
- **Ask Questions**
    -  Ask questions about your code in natural language. Our AI will provide relevant and accurate answers based on your repository.
- **Upload Audio**
    -  Upload meeting recordings to get AI-powered transcriptions and summaries of key points discussed in your meetings.
